    Ingrediemts

  • arugula
  • strawberries
  • grated parmesan cheese
  • pine nuts
  • cherry tomatoes
  • balsamic vinegar sauce
  • olive oil

    Steps

  1. Toss the ingredients in a big bowl.
  2. Add the olive oil, and salt and peper to your taste.
  3. Toss and turn until all ingredients are mixed properly and the taste is evenly spread in the bowl.
